The person behind the code

A quick snapshot of who I am and what I bring.

I'm a third-year CS Engineering student at Medicaps University, Indore with a strong interest in building clean, functional frontends and scalable systems. I started with curiosity and turned it into a habit — side projects, late-night debugging sessions, and an obsession with getting the UI just right.
